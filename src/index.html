Vote

How it works !

  • 1 Initiating Vote D-App and Smart Contract will be deployed by Election Commission on Blockchain Network
  • 2 Visit D-App Voter will open D-Vote Web App in Browser
  • 3 Wallet Login Voter will login to a Crypto-Wallet
  • 4 Vote Request The vote transaction will be done and 0.001 Ether Will be deducted from Voter's Wallet
  • 5 Vote authentication Votes are Authenticated,Encrypted and Added to Blockchain
  • 6 Voter anonymization Voters are Anonymization through a shuffling-based encryption process
  • 7 Voting Times Up After alloted time from Election Commission Votes will no longer be recorded
  • 8 Result time Final results scores can be viewed
